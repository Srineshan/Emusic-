Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page. Provide your email address, choose a password, and complete the registration process.

To change your password, go to the "Settings" page from your profile, select the "Change Password" option, and follow the prompts to update your password.

The Free plan allows basic features, with ads and limited skips. The Premium plan offers an ad-free experience, unlimited skips, and offline downloads. The Family plan allows sharing with up to 6 members.

To search for music, simply use the search bar at the top of the app. You can search for songs, artists, albums, or playlists.

To download songs, go to the song or playlist you want to save, and click the download icon next to it. You can access your downloaded content from the "Library" tab.

Ensure your Bluetooth speaker or device is turned on and in pairing mode. Then, go to the app's settings and select "Bluetooth" to connect your device. You can also use AirPlay for supported devices.

Yes, you can log in to the same account on multiple devices. Your playlists, preferences, and downloads will sync across all devices.

To delete your account, go to the "Settings" page, select "Account," and click on the "Delete Account" option. If you would like to request data removal, please contact our support team.
